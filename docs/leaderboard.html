📥 Install Tetris on your badge

1

Connect your badge via USB

Open a serial monitor (VS Code + Serial Monitor extension, PuTTY, or Arduino IDE) at 115200 baud.

2

Set your WiFi credentials

Type these commands in the serial monitor:

wifi-ssid YOUR_NETWORK_NAME
wifi-password YOUR_PASSWORD
3

Point badge to the Tetris manifest

This tells OnionOS where to download the game from:

scripts-url https://MysticOnChain.github.io/onion-tetris/manifest.json
4

Reboot and play!

Type reboot or unplug/replug. The badge downloads Tetris automatically and shows it in the launcher. Select it to start playing.

5

Submit your score

When your game ends, your badge shows a QR code. Scan it with your phone — it opens this leaderboard and records your score + wallet address automatically.

🎮 Badge Controls

LEFT Move piece left
RIGHT Move piece right
UP Rotate clockwise
DOWN Soft drop (1 row)
SELECT Hard drop (instant)

💡 Tip: The piece falls every 1 second at Level 1. Each 10 lines cleared = new level. At Level 10 the piece falls every 100ms — how far can you go?

300 × level. Stack tight, reward big.

Tetris (4 lines)

1200 × level. The only way to win on the leaderboard. Hold the I-piece.

📈 Level progression

  1. Every 10 lines cleared = level up
  2. Each level, the piece drops 100ms faster (starts at 1000ms, minimum 100ms)
  3. Higher level = more score per line. Play fast, score big.

❓ FAQ

How does the prize pool get funded?
The 175 ONION prize pool is funded each week from the OnionDAO community treasury. The pool is NOT held on this website — tokens are sent directly from the OnionDAO wallet to winners after each weekly reset. No entry fee, no buy-in, ever.
How do I make sure I receive my tokens?
Two ways work — whichever your badge has set up:

Option 1 — Onion ID (easiest): If your badge is linked to an OnionDAO profile, your Onion ID is submitted automatically. The organizer can send tokens directly on oniondao.dev using your ID — no wallet address needed.

Option 2 — Wallet address: If your badge has a Solana wallet configured, that address is submitted with your score. Tokens are sent directly to it. Check your wallet via the OnionOS serial console (wallet command).
Can I submit scores from multiple games?
Yes — submit up to 20 games per day. Only your single highest score from the week appears on the leaderboard. More games = more chances to set a record.
What if two badges tie on score?
In case of an exact tie on score, the tiebreaker is lines cleared (higher = better), then level reached, then earliest submission time. So submit your big score early in the week!
Can I see my own badge's score history?
Your badge ID (first 8 chars shown publicly) is tied to your hardware. You can recognize your entry by your badge ID — visible in the OnionOS serial console via the id command.
I don't have an OnionDAO badge. Can I still play?
You need a physical OnionDAO badge with OnionOS installed to participate in the contest. Check oniondao.dev to get yours!
The leaderboard shows my wallet address — is that safe?
Solana wallet addresses are public by design — they are safe to share. Only the first 14 characters are shown here. The full address is stored privately and used only for prize delivery.
